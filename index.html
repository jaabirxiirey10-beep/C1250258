Welcome to Arsenal

Arsenal Football Club was founded in 1886 and is one of the biggest football clubs in England. The club plays at the Emirates Stadium in North London. This website allows supporters to view the team, match schedule, gallery and become club members.

Tariiqda Arsenal

Taageerayaasha Arsenal waxay ku faanaan hal-ku-dhigga "Victoria Concordia Crescit", oo macnihiisu yahay "Guushu waxay ka timaaddaa wadajir." Rajadoodu waa inay kooxda sii arkaan iyadoo ku guuleysanaysa koobab badan mustaqbalka.

Qaab ciyaeredka Arsenal

Arsenal waxay caan ku tahay kubad qurux badan, ciyaar weerar ah, iyo horumarinta ciyaartoyda da'da yar. Kooxdu waxay ku tartantaa horyaalka England iyo tartamada Yurub xilli kasta.

Taagerashada Arsenal

Arsenal waa koox kubadda cagta oo ka dhisan Waqooyiga London. Waxay caan ku tahay taariikhdeeda weyn iyo taageerayaal ku kala nool dunida oo dhan. Kooxdu waxay ciyaarteeda ku martigelisaa Emirates Stadium.
